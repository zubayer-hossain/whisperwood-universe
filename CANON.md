# Whisperwood Canon

> **Authority:** This document is the highest authority of the Whisperwood Universe. When story, character, or world guidance conflicts with any other file in this repository, **CANON.md** prevails.

---

## Purpose

This document defines the **permanent canon** of the Whisperwood Universe.

It establishes what Whisperwood *is* — its identity, values, rules, and creative boundaries — so that every story, character, and world detail remains consistent across all formats and over time.

Canon is not a draft. Changes to this document are intentional acts of authorship. They must be made deliberately, documented clearly, and understood to affect the entire universe.

Contributors, collaborators, and AI assistants must read this document before creating or revising any Whisperwood material.

Whisperwood is a place where wonder feels natural, kindness has weight, and adventure invites young audiences to look closer at the world around them. Stories are told with warmth, clarity, and respect for the imagination of children.

## Core Themes

These themes are the emotional foundation of Whisperwood. They should be felt in every story, whether stated directly or woven quietly into the narrative.

- **Kindness creates magic** — compassion and care are never wasted; they open doors that force alone cannot
- **Curiosity leads to discovery** — asking questions and paying attention are acts of courage
- **Friendship is strength** — bonds between characters make hard moments survivable and good moments meaningful
- **Family comes first** — home, belonging, and the people who love you anchor every adventure
- **Nature should be respected** — the natural world is alive, valuable, and worthy of gentle stewardship
- **Every problem has a peaceful solution** — conflict may arise, but resolution favors understanding, cooperation, and care over harm

---

## Storytelling Principles

### Stories Should

Every Whisperwood story is an invitation. Each narrative should:

- **Inspire curiosity** — leave children wondering, noticing, and wanting to explore
- **Encourage kindness** — show characters choosing care, even when it is not the easiest path
- **Reward teamwork** — demonstrate that working together achieves what no one could alone
- **End with hope** — conclude on a note of warmth, possibility, or quiet joy
- **Teach without preaching** — let lessons emerge through action, consequence, and character; never lecture the audience

### Stories Must Never Include

Whisperwood protects its young audience. The following have no place in this universe:

- Horror
- Gore
- Cruelty
- Bullying as comedy
- Adult humor
- Politics
- Religion
- Real-world conflict

When creative choices sit near a boundary, choose the gentler path. The audience should always feel safe, seen, and uplifted.

## Character Rules

Characters are the heart of Whisperwood. Their consistency is non-negotiable.

**Continuity.** Characters must remain consistent across all stories and media. A character's personality, voice, values, and relationships are fixed unless this canon document is intentionally updated to reflect a deliberate change.

**Intentional evolution.** Character personalities cannot change without an authorized revision to canon. Growth is welcome; inconsistency is not.

**Humanity through imperfection.** Each character has strengths and weaknesses. No character is perfect. Flaws make characters relatable, teachable, and real — without making them unkind, cruel, or unsafe for children.

**Approval before alteration.** Established characters must not be modified, reimagined, or extended with new backstory without explicit approval.

---

## World Rules

These rules define how Whisperwood behaves as a world. They apply to every story set within the universe.

**Animals can communicate.** Animals in Whisperwood may speak, express emotion, and participate in stories as full characters. Communication should feel natural and wonder-filled, never unsettling.

**Children are always safe.** Young characters may face challenges, uncertainty, or gentle peril, but they are never placed in genuine danger. The audience must always trust that children in Whisperwood will be all right.

**Magic is gentle.** Magic in Whisperwood is soft, warm, and wondrous — never frightening, violent, or corrupting. It exists to delight and to deepen emotional truth, not to dominate or intimidate.

**Magic serves the story.** Magic exists to help stories, not to solve every problem. Characters should still think, cooperate, and grow. Wonder supports the narrative; it does not replace effort, kindness, or courage.

**Technology is simple and child-friendly.** Tools and devices in Whisperwood are approachable, understandable, and appropriate for young audiences. Technology supports daily life and adventure without complexity, cynicism, or coldness.

## Brand Vision

Whisperwood is a **timeless storytelling universe** — built to endure across generations and to grow thoughtfully over time.

The universe is designed to expand into books, animation, games, educational content, and interactive experiences. Every format must honor the same canon, the same themes, and the same commitment to children aged 4–8.

Whisperwood is not a trend. It is a world — one worth returning to, again and again, with kindness, curiosity, and hope.
