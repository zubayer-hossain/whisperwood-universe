# images

Canonical and production illustrations for this character.

---

## Purpose

Finished or approved artwork used in books, websites, Story Studio, marketing, and AI image generation reference.

---

## What Belongs Here

- Front and side portraits
- Expression sheets
- Full-body illustrations
- Approved production stills derived from final design

---

## What Does Not Belong Here

- Rough sketches → use [concepts/](../concepts/)
- Mood boards → use [references/](../references/)
- Animation frames → use [animation/](../animation/)

---

## Naming Convention

```
portrait-front.png
portrait-side.png
expressions-sheet.png
full-body-default.png
```

Use lowercase kebab-case. Be descriptive and consistent across characters.

---

## Linking

Register each canonical image in `profile.json` → `media` with appropriate `type` (`image` or `illustration`) and accessible `alt` text.
