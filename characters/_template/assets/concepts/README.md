# concepts

Concept art and design exploration for this character.

---

## Purpose

Early visual exploration before a design is approved as canonical. Concept art informs final illustrations but is not necessarily publication-ready.

---

## What Belongs Here

- Rough character sketches
- Color exploration
- Design iterations (version A, B, C)
- Notes from art review sessions (as images or PDFs)

---

## Guidelines

- Label files with version or date when multiple iterations exist (e.g. `concept-v1.png`, `concept-v2.png`)
- When a design is approved, move or recreate the final version in [images/](../images/)
- Concept art may be archived but should not be deleted without editorial approval

---

## For Illustrators

Use this folder during design phase. Once `status` in `profile.json` is `canonical`, canonical visuals should live in `images/`.
