# voice

Voice direction samples and audio references for this character.

---

## Purpose

Audio material for voice actors, audiobook production, and AI voice synthesis. Structured direction lives in `profile.json` → `voiceProfile`; this folder holds samples and extended reference.

---

## What Belongs Here

- Voice actor reference recordings
- Approved dialogue samples
- Pronunciation guides (audio)
- AI synthesis reference clips
- Direction notes as audio briefs (optional)

---

## Guidelines

- Preferred formats: WAV (production), MP3 (reference)
- Use descriptive filenames: `sample-line-greeting.wav`, `reference-tone-warm.wav`
- Keep clips short and labeled
- Ensure content is appropriate for ages 4–8

---

## Linking

Reference in `profile.json` → `media` with `type`: `audio` and descriptive `label`.

Expanded direction belongs in [profile.md](../../profile.md) → Voice Notes.

---

## For Voice Actors

Read `profile.md` → Voice Notes and `profile.json` → `voiceProfile` before recording. Match tone, energy, and vocabulary level defined in JSON.
